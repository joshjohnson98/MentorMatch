About

Want to learn tips from an industry pro? Want to help like-minded people accomplish their goals? Want to expand your professional network? You can do it all and more with Mentor Match. Mentor Match is a mentorship and advocacy matching service that connects users across industries and backgrounds in order to help them learn, grow, and achieve more.
